# Connections

The agent maps to these tools only. If a task needs a tool not listed here, ask before assuming it is connected. `map-connections` fills this table from the connectors actually installed. Three used beats twelve connected.

| Tool | Purpose | When to use | Example query |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Slack] | [Team comms and customer signal] | [When I ask what was said about a person, topic, or deal] | [What did the team say about the Q3 launch this week?] |
| [Notion] | [Docs, specs, pipeline] | [When I ask about a doc, spec, or pipeline status] | [Which pipeline items have no owner?] |
| [Linear] | [Engineering work] | [When I ask about issues, cycles, or delivery] | [What slipped from this cycle?] |
| [Calendar] | [Meetings and time] | [When I ask about my week or prep for a meeting] | [What do I have before the board meeting?] |
